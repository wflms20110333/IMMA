about browserbug

Browserbug is a custom companion to provide messages of motivation and encouragement!

Whether you'd like your favorite music idol to give you a pep talk or an old friend to remind you to take a break, Browserbugs make working virtually a more productive and less lonely experience.

Download the Chrome extension here: we're currently working on a bunch of cool new features, so subscribe to our Facebook or Twitter to get sneak peeks of what's coming up next!

As college students, we've struggled to manage our browsing habits, and in quarantine, we've felt more isolated than ever. So we were inspired to create Browserbug: we hope that it brings a spark of life to your online work as well.

We'd love to hear your suggestions on how to improve! If you've enjoyed Browserbug, please help us spread the word! And feel free to buy us some Ko-Fi so we can continue developing Browserbug to become better and better :)
