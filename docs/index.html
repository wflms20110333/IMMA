About IMMA


IMMA, or Intelligent Motivation Managing Application, is a browser extension that provides you with a customizable AI friend to accompany you and provide messages of encouragement!

Whether you'd like Shia LaBeouf to give you a pep talk or Pusheen to remind you to take a break, or if you'd like to create your very own AI character, IMMAs will make working virtually a more productive and less lonely experience for fans!

We'd like to learn about the best ways for IMMA characters to interact with users, and which characters you would be interested in seeing -- fill out our survey here.
