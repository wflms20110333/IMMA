About Browserbug


Browserbug is a browser extension that provides you with a customizable friend to accompany you and provide messages of encouragement!

Whether you'd like Shia LaBeouf to give you a pep talk or Pusheen to remind you to take a break, or if you'd like to create your very own character, browserbugs will make working virtually a more productive and less lonely experience for fans!

We'd like to learn about the best ways for browserbugs to interact with users, and which characters you would be interested in seeing -- fill out our survey here.
