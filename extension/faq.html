Troubleshooting

Not receiving messages?

Check that your device isn't set to "do not disturb," "focus assist," or otherwise blocking notifications.

My messages stay on my screen for too long / my messages fade too quickly.

Notification duration is controlled by your operating system. For example, in Windows 10, you can control this in Ease of Access > Display > Show notifications for.... You can also disable notification fading through the Options page.
