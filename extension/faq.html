FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

TROUBLESHOOTING

I don't see the Browserbug icon in my extensions bar?

Click the puzzle piece icon or see if Browserbug might be hidden or minimized!

I'm not receiving my messages!

This is the most common issue users have! Don't panic: you can fix this in just a few seconds.

For Windows 10 users:

  1. Is Focus Assist turned on?
    • Open the Action Center to check! (Windows logo key + A)
    • Focus Assist will sometimes block notifications: if you want to use Browserbug during Focus Assist, you should add Google Chrome to your priority list.
  2. Are your messages being kept in the Action Center?
    • Open the Action Center to check! (Windows logo key + A)
    • If your notifications are being saved to the Action Center instead of being shown on screen, you can disable this. Open System > Notifications & Actions > Google Chrome and uncheck "Show notification in action center."
  3. Are notifications turned on?
    • Open System > Notifications & Actions
    • To receive messages, notifications should be allowed, and Google Chrome notifications should be allowed.
  4. Is Chrome "quieter messaging" turned on?
    • Open Notifications at chrome://settings/content/notifications
    • Try turning off quieter messaging.
  5. To test if messages are now working, you can increase message frequency in Options.

If you haven't received a message in a while, opening the popup menu (click icon in extensions bar) will check for and replace any missed message timers.

If you're using a different operating system or are still having problems receiving messages, feel free to reach out to us!
