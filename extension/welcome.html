How to Use Browserbug

Welcome to Browserbug! We've already loaded a default character, "Browserbee," for you to use :)

By now, you should have already received a notification from it. (If not, check out our Troubleshooting section!)

When you're ready, feel free to visit the Studio and start designing your own character!

For more advanced settings (want to adjust how often you get messages?) visit the Options page.

Thanks for installing Browserbug! We hope you enjoy it :) Feel free to send us a [[ko-fi]] so we can keep developing!

Privacy

To preserve your privacy, we keep information locally on your own computer whenever possible, minimizing what's visible to us.

Feel free to check out our Privacy Policy for more details!
